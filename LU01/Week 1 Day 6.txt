Prompting:

Prompt Engineering: The art of refining inputs to get optimal AI outputs.
Prompt engineering is power but it should never be an excuse for AI flaws.
Anytime critics point out issues defenders "always say you're prompting wrong"

The prompt framework (T-C-R-E-I)
1. Week 1 Day 1.txt1. Task
	Persona: ex. Marketer, Teacher
	Format: Output type
2. Context
	Good: "Ideas for a gift under $30"
	Great: "5 ideas for a 29-year-old who loves winter sports and just switched from snowboarding 
	to skiing. Budget: $30"
3. Reference
	Provide data, example or specific ideas
	Attach documents
4. Evaluate
	Review the output
5. Iterate
	Feed errors back in
	Add more detail or refine constraints
