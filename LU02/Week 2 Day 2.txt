Rag  - Retrieval Augmented Generation
- An AI framework that improves the accuracy and relevance of LLMs by fetching trusted, external data before 
generating a response.
- Very cost efficient.
- Can train yourself 

I used Notebook RAG to create two different outputs. First, I generated a Svelte work example. Then I created an informational piece about sunfish using the same retrieval-augmented process.
